B-Brad & Associates

B-Brad offers exceptional, reliable, and timely brokerage services to hundreds of shippers and carriers, specializing in Full Truckloads (FTL), Less Than Truckloads (LTL), and Partial Truckloads (PTL) freight transports throughout North America.

We offer fast and friendly professional freight arrangement services throughout North America. Our model places you first and ensures all of your needs are met, by handling your transportation needs in a professional and customer-friendly manner every time. We know that transportation issues can grind companies to a halt and understand that every shipper has a unique set of needs.

Our fast and friendly team is up for any job, big or small. We will deliver mostly Anywhere, Anytime, and On-time. Our customers know that we stand behind our work, putting your satisfaction as our #1 priority.
