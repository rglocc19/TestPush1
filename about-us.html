About Us

B-Brad & Associates

B-Brad understands that every shipper has a unique set of needs, and we will do whatever is necessary to meet those needs while maintaining the highest caliber of support.

Fast and Friendly, we offer professional freight arrangement services throughout North America. Our team is up for any job, big or small, we will deliver Anywhere, Anytime, and On-time. Our customers know that we stand behind our work, putting your satisfaction as our #1 priority. What really makes us stand out is our dedication, competitive pricing and attention to detail.

Provide logistical services in the most professional and proactive manner and deliver those services in a friendly, no-nonsense, customer-focused approach that will set B-Brad & Associate Logistics apart from all other organizations in the supply chain industry.

Our Mission

We handle the logistical needs of extremely large corporations to small, privately-owned companies. Your freight is treated with equal importance regardless if it is going ten miles or 1,000 miles.

Our Vision

B-Brad Logistics model places you first and ensures all of your needs are met

Our Goal

B-Brad Logistics specializes in FTL, LTL and PTL freight transports throughout North America.
